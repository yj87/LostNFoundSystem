About the System

The Lost & Found System is a comprehensive digital platform designed specifically for Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM) students, faculty, and staff. Our system bridges the gap between those who lose items and those who find them, making the reunion process seamless and transparent.

Our platform handles everything from smartphones and laptops to wallets, IDs, and personal accessories. The system features real-time reporting, automated matching algorithms, secure claim verification, and direct communication between finders and owners. All personal information is protected with industry-standard encryption.
