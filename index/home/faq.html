General Questions

The Lost & Found System is a centralized platform for Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM) students and staff to report lost items and browse found belongings. Our mission is to help the UTM community reunite with their valuable possessions quickly and securely.

Yes! The Lost & Found System is completely free for all UTM students and staff. There are no hidden fees or charges for reporting lost items, browsing found items, or claiming your belongings.

The system is exclusively available to current UTM students, faculty members, and staff. You need a valid UTM email address to register and access all features of the platform.

Reporting Found Items

To report a found item, log into your account, click on "Report Found Item", fill in the item details including category, description, location found, and upload a photo if available. Submit the form and the item will be listed for the owner to find.

Please include as much detail as possible: item category (electronics, books, accessories, etc.), brand/color, specific description, exact location where found, date found, and a clear photo of the item. This helps the rightful owner identify their belongings.

Yes, you can edit or delete your report at any time. Go to "My Reports" in your dashboard, find the item, and click on "Edit" or "Delete". If the item has been claimed, you may no longer be able to edit it.

Claiming Items

Browse the "Found Items" section to see all reported items. When you find an item that belongs to you, click "Claim Item" and fill in the claim request form with proof of ownership. The reporter will be notified and can approve your claim.

Provide any proof that shows the item belongs to you - photos, serial numbers, receipts, unique markings, or describing specific details only the owner would know. This helps prevent false claims.

Once you submit a claim request, the reporter has up to 7 days to respond. Most claims are resolved within 2-3 days. You will receive email notifications about your claim status.

Account & Security

Click the "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your details including your UTM email address, full name, and create a password. Verify your email address and you're ready to use the system.

On the login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Yes! We take your privacy seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We only share your contact information with the relevant parties when necessary for the claim process.
